Research

My research lies in natural language processing and computational social science, with a focus on how conversational dynamics shape participation, equity, and experience in online and video-chat settings.

I am especially interested in developing computational methods that make these interaction patterns legible, so that we can design tools and interventions that support healthier, more intentional conversations.
