Research

Conversations are really cool!
They reveal how people build ideas, negotiate disagreements, and shape relationships through interaction. Dialogue isn’t just talking, it’s the infrastructure of social life, powering collaboration, conflict, persuasion, and learning. By modeling how conversations unfold over time, we can better understand human behavior and preferences. This helps us design technologies and models that participate in dialogue more naturally and responsibly, supporting human's social interactions.
